Data Driven Perspective on Nicotine pouches

The objective of this analysis is to provide recommendations using data driven decision making and leverage sales and revenue data to optimize marketing strategies, pricing decisions and product offerings for nicotine pouches.

The Analysis aims to answer the following questions:

  • What is the best time of a month to run a consumer offer campaign?
  • What multi flavor bundle shall we come up with?
  • Apart from Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad and Peshawar what other cities we shall focus and why?
  • After the induction of watermelon flavor, do we see increase in 11mg uptake in overall share?
  • Do we see any decline in volumes, after the price increase in July?
